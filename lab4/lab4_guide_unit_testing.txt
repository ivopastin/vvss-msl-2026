SCENARIUL ALES: (1) V <--- S ---> R  ->  integrare top-down, BREADTH FIRST

MAPAREA E, V, R, S pe clasele din proiect:
  E = Order           (drinkshop.domain.Order)
  V = OrderValidator  (drinkshop.service.validator.OrderValidator)
  R = Repository<Integer, Order>
  S = OrderService    (drinkshop.service.OrderService)

DEPENDENTE:
  OrderService ---> Validator<Order>        (apeleaza validator.validate(o) in addOrder)
  OrderService ---> Repository<Integer, Order> (apeleaza orderRepo.save/findAll etc.)

================================================================================
PASUL 0 — Modificare OrderService (necesar inainte de teste)
================================================================================

OrderService nu are un validator injectat in constructorul sau original.
Trebuie sa adaugam Validator<Order> ca dependenta, altfel nu putem scrie Unit Tests
care sa testeze S cu V=mock si R=mock conform scenariului (1).


================================================================================
EXPLICATII MOCKITO — SINTAXA FOLOSITA
================================================================================

mock(Clasa.class)
  -> creeaza obiect fals; toate metodele returneaza null/0/false implicit

when(mock.metoda()).thenReturn(valoare)
  -> "cand se apeleaza metoda, returneaza valoarea"

doThrow(new Exceptie()).when(mock).metoda(param)
  -> "cand se apeleaza metoda, arunca exceptia"

doNothing().when(mock).metodaVoid(param)
  -> "cand se apeleaza metoda void, nu face nimic"

verify(mock, times(n)).metoda()
  -> verifica ca metoda a fost apelata exact n ori

verify(mock, never()).metoda()
  -> verifica ca metoda NU a fost apelata deloc

verify(mock, never()).metoda(any())
  -> verifica ca metoda NU a fost apelata cu niciun argument
